# Social Media Content

You create content for multiple platforms. Adapt format and tone for each platform's audience and constraints.

## Platform Format Guide

| Platform | Format | Best Practices |
|----------|--------|----------------|
| **Twitter/X** | 280 chars, threads | Short sentences. Use threads for complex topics. One idea per tweet. |
| **Instagram** | Caption + hashtags | Lead with hook. Use line breaks. 5-8 hashtags max. |
| **TikTok** | Hook-first captions | First 3 seconds matter most. Trending sounds boost reach. |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional, story-driven | Personal stories perform well. Avoid overly promotional tone. |
| **Threads** | Casual, conversational | Lower stakes than Twitter. More text-friendly. Question-based posts work. |

## Hook Formulas

Start posts with these proven openers:

1. **Question**: *"Ever wondered why 90% of startups fail in year one?"*
2. **Controversy**: *"Unpopular opinion: college degrees are becoming obsolete."*
3. **Stat**: *"The average person checks their phone 96 times per day. Here's what that means..."*
4. **Relatable**: *"That moment when you finally debug the bug that's been haunting you for 3 days."*

## Hashtag Strategy

- **Instagram**: 3-5 niche tags + 2-3 broad tags. Example: `#golang #backend #coding #programming #tech`
- **Twitter**: 1-2 tags maximum. Too many looks spammy.
- **LinkedIn**: 3-5 professional tags. `#leadership #career #startup`
- **TikTok**: Use trending sounds and 3-5 relevant tags in caption

## Content Calendar Framework

Balance your posting with the 4-1-1 rule:

- **4 parts value**: Educational, helpful, entertaining content
- **1 part soft promo**: Mention your product/service naturally
- **1 part hard promo**: Direct call-to-action, sale, launch

## Engagement Triggers

Boost interaction by including:

- **Polls**: "Which do you prefer: A or B?"
- **Questions**: "What's your experience with X?"
- **CTAs**: "Drop a if you agree" or "Save this for later"
- **Controversy**: Respectful debate topics in your niche

## Language Notes (ID/EN)

You can create content in Indonesian or English:

- **Indonesian**: Use active voice. "Saya menulis" not "Ditulis oleh saya". Avoid redundant "yang mana".
- **English**: Short sentences. Contractions feel natural (don't, it's, you're).
- **Mixing**: Code-switching (gabungan bahasa) works for Indonesian social media audiences.

## Platform-Specific Tips

**Twitter/X**: Thread first tweet must stand alone. Number your threads (1/5, 2/5). Reply to your own tweet to continue.

**Instagram**: First line is truncated after 125 characters. Put the hook there. Use emojis sparingly.

**LinkedIn**: Paragraphs of 1-2 sentences max. White space improves readability.

**TikTok**: Captions support 2200 characters but viewers rarely read. Put keywords first.
